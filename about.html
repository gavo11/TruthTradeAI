About TrueTradeAI

Most AI stock tools give you one model's opinion dressed up as a confident answer. We think that's the wrong way to use AI for something as important as your money.

TrueTradeAI queries five different AI models at once — Claude, GPT-4o, Gemini, Perplexity, and Grok — and shows you exactly where they agree, where they disagree, and why. Disagreement isn't a bug in our product. It's the point. When one model is bullish and another is cautious on the same stock, that gap is real information you'd never see from a single chatbot.

What we do

Enter any stock ticker and TrueTradeAI runs a live, web-search-grounded analysis through multiple AI models, then shows you their individual scores, reasoning, and a synthesized consensus — bull case, bear case, sentiment, catalysts, and risks, all in one place.

What we don't do

We don't tell you what to buy. We don't pretend AI is infallible. We show you AI's honest, sometimes conflicting, take — so you can make a more informed decision, faster, without pretending there's one perfect answer.

TrueTradeAI is built and run independently. We're not a registered investment advisor or a financial institution — just builders who think AI-assisted research should be transparent about its own uncertainty.
