Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains what information TrueTradeAI ("we," "us," or "our") collects, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information when you use truetradeai.com (the "Service").

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information:

CategoryExamplesSource
Account informationEmail address, password (encrypted)Provided by you at signup
Usage dataTickers analyzed, number of daily analyses, timestampsCollected automatically
Payment informationSubscription status, billing historyProcessed by Stripe — we never see or store your full card details
Technical dataIP address (used to enforce free-tier usage limits for non-account users)Collected automatically
Watchlist dataTickers you save to your watchlistStored locally in your browser, tied to your account

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the Service, including authenticating your account and enforcing usage limits
  • To process payments and manage your subscription via Stripe
  • To communicate with you about your account, billing, or changes to our Service or policies
  • To improve the Service and diagnose technical issues
  • To prevent fraud, abuse, and violations of our Terms of Service

4. Third-Party Services

We rely on third-party providers to operate the Service. Each processes data according to their own privacy policies:

  • Supabase — authentication and database hosting
  • Stripe — payment processing and subscription billing
  • Google (Gemini), OpenAI, Anthropic, Perplexity, xAI (Grok) — AI analysis generation. Stock tickers you search are sent to these providers to generate analysis; we do not send your account email or personal identity to these providers as part of analysis requests.
  • Vercel — website hosting

We do not sell your personal information to third parties, and we do not use your data to train AI models.

5. Data Retention

We retain account information for as long as your account remains active. Usage data used for free-tier limit enforcement is retained on a rolling daily basis. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or export your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe.

7. Cookies and Local Storage

We use browser local storage to save your watchlist and session information. We do not use third-party advertising trackers or cookies for ad targeting.

8. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, contact us and we will delete it.

9. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including encrypted password storage and secure third-party infrastructure. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the Service after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

11. Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy or your data can be directed to truetradeaisupport@gmail.com.
